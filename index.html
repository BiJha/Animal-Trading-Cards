German Shepherd Dog

Confident, Courageous, Smart

  • Height : 24-26 inches (male), 22-24 inches(female)
  • Weight : 65-90 pounds (male), 50-70 pounds(female)
  • Life Expectancy : 7-10 years
  • Group : Herding Group

There are many reasons why German Shepherds stand in the front rank of canine royalty, but experts say their defining attribute is character: loyalty, courage, confidence, the ability to learn commands for many tasks, and the willingness to put their life on the line in defense of loved ones. German Shepherds will be gentle family pets and steadfast guardians.
